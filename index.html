Hello, welcome to my homepage :)!

My name is Weijieying Ren, I also generally use the name as `Xiaoying Ren' in my research and daily life for its simplicity. I'm PhD student at PSU in the Artificial Intelligence group, super lucky to be advised by my best advisor Dr.Honavar .

I obtain my bacholar from HeFei University of Technology (HFUT) and master degree from University of Science and Technology (USTC), respectively. After that, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer in Singapore for approximately two years. From Jan. 2021, I returned to campus to pursue my Ph.D. degree.

My research interests include topics in machine learning and data mining. Most recently, I am interested in the following topics:

  1. Generalizable Machine Learning in Open Environments:
    • Knowledge adaptation with weak/inexact supervision.
    • knowledge memorization with out-of-distribution (OOD) data.
    • Knowledge adaptation with heteregeneous/complex relational data.
  2. Application with clinical data, considering its multi-modality, heterogeneous property.
    • Evaluation of continual learning with LLMs. (ongoing projects and my current focus)
    • Multi-modality modeling with Clinic notes, image and longitudinal data. (ongoing projects and my current focus)
